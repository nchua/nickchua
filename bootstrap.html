About me

Hi! I'm Max

I am currently a fourth year at UChicago studying computer science and an undergraduate research assistant at SAND Lab.

I am passionate about technology and startups and believe that being an entrepreneur is not a title but rather a mindset when approaching processes and challenges. Specifically, I am interested in the applications of machine learning, mobile computing, and computer vision in understanding the world around us. I envision a future where users will be able to seamlessly interact with both the real and the virtual, not limited by the screen size of their devices. Some areas that I think could make this possible are AR/VR/MR, edge computing, spatial computing, mobile sensing, and smart cities, among many others. Right now I am particularly fascinated by developments in AR.
